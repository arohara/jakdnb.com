Our advanced design build method acts as a single trusted source which integrates and stage- manages all services from consultation, architectural design and construction. Our process is timed and we keep tabs on our contractors and deadlines all the while having our customers in the loop. This means that the entire project is handled by a single entity which helps avoid expensive errors, confusing conversations and contracts with architects, managers and engineers. Design Build makes sure that the entire project flow is smooth, and ensures the best result on time, for your budget.

Whether building a new home interior or an urban office space redesigning, our uncompromising expertise is at your disposal. Most of all it’s your vision. We believe that our cutting edge architectural principles and strategies will not just fulfil our client’s requirements, but also provide them with an aesthetically pleasing and high-quality result. Our dream is this - constructing dream buildings for our happy clients.

The Construction Management method was built around the 1960’s in an attempt to develop an increased project value than the traditional general contracting method. At JAK, we handle your project from the initial to the final stage by assuming risks, sourcing quality materials, and under the supervision of our efficient crew of trained staff all the way from designing and completing your building. We undertake small to mid-sized projects in both the residential and commercial markets, managing them within a set time frame.

Our sustainable buildings come with an ecological design which makes sure that our built spaces minimize the detrimental impacts on the environment. We make this plausible by implying materials, design methods and energy in a way that they do not disturb the balance of our presently available natural resource. We additionally imply strategies such as natural light optimization, indoor temperature regulation and also providing the site selection service.

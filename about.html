About us

A few reason you should choose us

JAK Design’s & Build’s is a premier architect-led firm committed to providing superior quality design-build construction services up and down India. With a team of talented and experienced custom home creators and architectural professionals, we provide tailored services to the unique requirements of our clients.

We specialize in projects from constructing cozy urban homes, to designing sophisticated commercial interiors, drawing inspiration from the current architectural style in the country. We take pride in being the first ever company to launch the “Container building” project in Chennai.

Trust is the foundation... your vision is built

JAK Design’s & Build’s team of professional and talented architects, superintendents, and project managers are dedicated to provide a high standard of excellence in all aspects of the construction process. As we expand our footprint, we remain dedicated to the basis of our commitment to delivering superior quality design architecture, and efficient construction services. The relationship we have established with our stakeholders and clients is based on the confidence and mutual trust built over the years. JAK is the choice for the distinctive owners envisioning a masterly-artful project.

Economically built. Efficient homes

Building a dream home is expensive. To make this one-time investment a cost-effective one, it takes time and the right floor plan. JAK Design’s and Build’s well-qualified and reputable team has the skill and niche to optimize the home construction process as an affordable one. Our crew gathers your open requirements while creatively and strategically sources products and materials, paying attention to installation, material limitation and energy regulation. This optimized construction planning is highly regarded to create a properly devised and affordable designed home.

Leadership

Our Founder, Mr. Arun Kumar, is an architect with a postgraduate degree in Project Management and Construction Planning from the University of Central Lancashire, Preston, UK, and also holds a Masters of Research. Raised in a family of architects, our founder had the thirst to create something of his own, landing him to construct the JAK group. With the reputation and credibility of completing several major projects, JAK Design’s and Build’s now has footprints all over India.
